Why Manzanita?

Manzanita wood is loved for its twisting shapes, rich reddish-brown tones, and sturdy natural structure. It works beautifully in décor, terrariums, aquariums, bird perches, floral arrangements, and craft projects.

Care & Prep

For Aquariums

Rinse well and soak before use. Boiling or extended soaking can help reduce floating and tannins.

For Reptiles

Inspect for sharp points and secure branches safely inside the enclosure before adding animals.

For Décor

Dust gently with a soft cloth. Keep natural wood away from constant moisture unless prepared for it.
